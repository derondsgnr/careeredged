### SECTION 1: WHAT'S BROKEN

**Eyebrow:** The truth

**Headline:**
You were never given the tools.
You were given a search bar.

**Body:**
Think about it.

You spent years in education — and graduated without a career plan. You entered the job market with a resume and a prayer. You were told to "network" and "find your passion" by people who couldn't tell you what that means in practice.

The $11B career services industry optimizes for one thing: matching keywords to job descriptions. Not building careers. Not giving direction. Not knowing you.

Every month you spend drifting is a month you could have spent building.

### SECTION 2: WHAT SHOULD EXIST

**Eyebrow:** What we built instead

**Headline:**
A career platform that starts with you — not a job listing.

**Card 1 — A roadmap, not a results page**
Enter where you are. See where you could go. Get a phased plan with milestones, timelines, and skill requirements — structured around your life, not a job description.

**Card 2 — A guide, not a chatbot**
Someone who knows your industry, your level, and your goals. Who helps you build your resume, prep for interviews, and find the right opportunities — not just any opportunities.

**Card 3 — A system, not a one-time fix**
Your roadmap evolves as you do. Complete a phase, unlock the next. Change direction, the plan recalibrates. This isn't a quiz result you screenshot and forget.
