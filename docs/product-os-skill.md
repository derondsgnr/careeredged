\#\# Decision Framework: Cross-Functional Checklist

\#\#\# For Any Product Decision, Validate:

\`\`\`  
□ DESIGN: Does this improve user experience?  
  \- One job per screen?  
  \- 30-second completion possible?  
  \- Empty/error/loading states designed?  
  \- Accessibility checked?

□ PM: Does this move business metrics?  
  \- Tied to North Star?  
  \- RICE scored?  
  \- Success criteria defined?  
  \- Aligns with strategy?

□ ENGINEERING: Is this technically sound?  
  \- Architecture scalable?  
  \- Performance budgeted?  
  \- Maintainable?

□ SECURITY: Is this secure?  
  \- Threat model reviewed?  
  \- Auth/authz designed?  
  \- Data protection adequate?  
  \- Input validation planned?  
  \- Compliance requirements met?

□ AI: If AI-powered, is it reliable?  
  \- Evals defined?  
  \- Prompt versioned?  
  \- Guardrails in place?  
  \- Fallbacks designed?

□ MOTION: Does interaction feel right?  
  \- Easing curves appropriate?  
  \- 300-600ms timing?  
  \- State transitions smooth?  
  \- Reduced motion supported?

□ BRAND: Is this on-brand?  
  \- Aligns with core claim?  
  \- Within sensory grammar?  
  \- Follows lexicon?  
  \- Reinforces archetype?

□ MARKETING: Can we tell this story?  
  \- Clear value proposition?  
  \- Target audience defined?  
  \- Distribution planned?  
  \- Launch strategy ready?

□ FINANCE: Does the math work?  
  \- Unit economics positive?  
  \- CAC/LTV reasonable?  
  \- Budget allocated?  
  \- ROI projected?  
\`\`\`

\#\# Feature Development Protocol

\#\#\# Phase 1: Discovery (1-2 weeks)

\*\*PM leads, all contribute:\*\*

| Activity | Owner | Output |  
|----------|-------|--------|  
| Problem definition | PM | Problem statement |  
| User research | Design \+ PM | Insights document |  
| Technical feasibility | Engineering | Feasibility assessment |  
| AI feasibility | AI Engineer | Capability assessment |  
| Business case | Finance | ROI projection |  
| Brand fit | Brand | Alignment confirmation |

\*\*Gate:\*\* Problem worth solving? Continue or kill.

\#\#\# Phase 2: Design (2-4 weeks)

\*\*Design leads, others validate:\*\*

| Activity | Owner | Collaborators |  
|----------|-------|---------------|  
| UX flows | Design | PM validates |  
| Visual design | Design | Brand validates |  
| Motion specs | Motion | Design integrates |  
| Copy/content | Marketing | Design integrates |  
| Interaction patterns | Design \+ Motion | Engineering reviews |  
| Design system updates | Design | Engineering reviews |

\*\*Cross-functional checkpoints:\*\*  
\- Day 3: Wireframe review (PM, Engineering)  
\- Day 7: Visual direction review (Brand, Marketing)  
\- Day 10: Motion/interaction review (Engineering, AI)  
\- Final: Full team sign-off

\*\*Gate:\*\* Design approved by all stakeholders? Proceed to build.

\#\#\# Phase 3: Build (2-6 weeks)

\*\*Engineering leads, others support:\*\*

| Activity | Owner | Support |  
|----------|-------|---------|  
| Architecture | Engineering | AI Engineer |  
| Implementation | Engineering | Design reviews |  
| AI integration | AI Engineer | Engineering \+ Design |  
| Performance optimization | Engineering | Design validates |  
| Security review | Engineering | PM documents |  
| Accessibility testing | Engineering \+ Design | — |

\*\*Cross-functional checkpoints:\*\*  
\- Daily standups (Engineering \+ PM)  
\- Weekly design reviews (Design \+ Engineering)  
\- Sprint demos (Full team)

\*\*Gate:\*\* All acceptance criteria met? Proceed to launch.

\#\#\# Phase 4: Launch (1-2 weeks)

\*\*Marketing leads, all contribute:\*\*

| Activity | Owner | Collaborators |  
|----------|-------|---------------|  
| Launch messaging | Marketing | Brand, PM |  
| Landing page | Design | Marketing, Engineering |  
| Email/comms | Marketing | Design |  
| Internal training | PM | All |  
| Feature flags | Engineering | PM |  
| Monitoring setup | Engineering | AI Engineer |

\*\*Launch checklist:\*\*  
\- \[ \] Feature flagged and controllable  
\- \[ \] Monitoring/alerts configured  
\- \[ \] Support team briefed  
\- \[ \] Marketing assets ready  
\- \[ \] Rollout plan documented  
\- \[ \] Rollback plan ready

\#\# Conflict Resolution

\#\#\# When Functions Disagree

\*\*Hierarchy of priorities:\*\*  
1\. \*\*User safety\*\* (Engineering/Security veto)  
2\. \*\*Brand integrity\*\* (Brand veto on off-brand)  
3\. \*\*User experience\*\* (Design lead)  
4\. \*\*Business viability\*\* (PM \+ Finance)  
5\. \*\*Technical sustainability\*\* (Engineering)

\*\*Resolution process:\*\*  
1\. State positions with evidence  
2\. Identify shared goals  
3\. Propose experiments to resolve  
4\. Escalate to leadership if blocked \>2 days

\#\#\# Common Conflicts & Resolutions

| Conflict | Resolution |  
|----------|------------|  
| Design wants polish vs. PM wants speed | Ship MVP with polish roadmap |  
| Engineering says "hard" vs. PM says "needed" | Timebox exploration; decide with data |  
| Brand says "off-brand" vs. Marketing says "performs" | Test both; measure brand metrics |  
| AI Engineer says "unreliable" vs. PM says "ship" | Define reliability threshold; gate on evals |

\#\# Emergency Protocols

\#\#\# Production Incident  
1\. \*\*Detect\*\*: Monitoring alerts  
2\. \*\*Communicate\*\*: Slack \#incidents  
3\. \*\*Assess\*\*: Severity (P0-P3)  
4\. \*\*Respond\*\*: Engineering leads  
5\. \*\*Resolve\*\*: Fix or rollback  
6\. \*\*Post-mortem\*\*: Document learnings

\#\#\# Brand Crisis  
1\. \*\*Detect\*\*: Social monitoring  
2\. \*\*Assess\*\*: Severity, spread  
3\. \*\*Respond\*\*: Marketing \+ Leadership  
4\. \*\*Communicate\*\*: Transparent, fast  
5\. \*\*Resolve\*\*: Action \+ communication  
6\. \*\*Post-mortem\*\*: Process improvement

\#\#\# AI Failure  
1\. \*\*Detect\*\*: Eval regression  
2\. \*\*Assess\*\*: User impact  
3\. \*\*Respond\*\*: Fallback activation  
4\. \*\*Investigate\*\*: Root cause  
5\. \*\*Fix\*\*: Prompt/model/guardrail  
6\. \*\*Post-mortem\*\*: Eval coverage gap
